How commission works

Simple, honest, recurring.

Earn 50% MRR from referred customers while they stay paid and you keep an active Junior subscription.

  • To earn commission, you hold an active paid Junior plan — Solo or up qualifies.
  • You earn only when a referred customer becomes a paid customer — never on a free or trial signup.
  • Commission is 50% of their subscription, recurring for as long as they stay paid.
  • If your Junior subscription lapses, future commissions pause. Reactivate and eligible commissions resume from that point.
  • Already-paid commissions are never clawed back — except for fraud or abuse.
  • Payouts are managed by Whop and paid in 170+ countries.

First-touch attribution is locked at signup and is never reassigned. Affiliate IDs are Whop affiliate records. Junior may pause an affiliate's offer for abuse.

Questions

Affiliate FAQ.

Who can join?

Anyone with an audience of clippers or creators. Sign in with Whop to get your tracked link. Commission payouts require an active paid Junior subscription (Solo or up).

When do I get paid?

Commission starts after your referred customer's first successful paid invoice, then recurs each month they stay paid. Whop handles the payouts.

What happens if I cancel my Junior plan?

Future commissions pause while your subscription is inactive. Reactivate and eligible commissions resume from that point forward. Commissions already paid to you are yours to keep.

What's the starter pass my audience gets?

Approved invite links can unlock 100 reward clip exports before Solo billing starts. New clippers add a card and bring their own OpenAI key. (Rolling out — ask in the partner dashboard.)

Does Junior submit clips to Whop for them?

No. Junior prepares submission-ready clips and helps track the submission link — clippers post and submit on Whop themselves. Whop tracks views and pays out.

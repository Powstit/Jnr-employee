## 2. Junior Pricing — Restructured for $1M MRR

Current tiers are too low for the value delivered. A professional clipper who posts 10 clips/day across 10 accounts saves 3–5 hours daily. They'll pay more than $29.

### Proposed Tiers (Annual Discount = 2 Months Free)

| Tier | Monthly | Annual (billed yearly) | What They Get | Ayrshare Cost/User (at 10K scale) | Gross Margin |
|------|---------|------------------------|---------------|-----------------------------------|--------------|
| **Free** | $0 | $0 | 3 clips, no publish, no schedule | $0 | — |
| **Solo** | $39 | $390 | 50 clips/mo, 1 platform publish, no schedule | $2.87 | **92.6%** |
| **Growth** | $79 | $790 | 200 clips/mo, multi-platform publish, schedule 30 days | $2.87 | **96.4%** |
| **Autopilot** | $149 | $1,490 | Unlimited clips, multi-platform, schedule + drip (1-4 weeks), analytics | $2.87 | **98.1%** |
| **Agency** | $299 | $2,990 | Everything + sub-accounts (5 team members), white-label exports, priority support | $2.87 | **99.0%** |

### Why These Prices Work

- **Solo $39:** A clipper buying 1 coffee/day spends more than this. One viral clip pays for 6 months.
- **Growth $79:** Scheduling alone saves 2 hours/week. At minimum wage that's $58/week = $232/mo value.
- **Autopilot $149:** Drip posting (auto-spaced across weeks) is a premium feature no competitor offers at this price. Agencies charge $500-2,000/mo for this.
- **Agency $299:** Clippers with teams (editors, VA's, managers) need this. Comparable social management tools (Hootsuite $249+/seat, Sprout Social $249+/seat) are more expensive per user.

## 5. The Moat — Why This Is Defensible

### Ayrshare alone doesn't do what Junior does

Ayrshare is an API. It posts content. It doesn't:
- Download YouTube videos
- Transcribe audio with AI
- Find the best viral moments
- Cut clips automatically
- Generate captions with AI
- Add animated subtitles
- Resize for platform specs (9:16, 1:1, 16:9)
- Reformat for different platforms

### Junior alone doesn't post

Junior clips beautifully. But without Ayrshare, the user still has to manually upload to every platform. That's the 3-hour daily pain point.

### Combined: The only end-to-end "drop video → viral clips → all platforms" tool

Nobody else does this. The competitors:

| Tool | What they do | What they DON'T do |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| **OpusClip / Vizard** | AI clip selection | No auto-posting. Export + manual upload. |
| **Hootsuite / Buffer** | Schedule + post | No AI clipping. You bring the content. |
| **CapCut** | Edit + basic caption | No AI selection. No auto-posting to 10 accounts. |
| **Descript** | Edit by transcript | No clip selection. No auto-posting. |
| **Repurpose.io** | Resize + repost | No AI clip selection. Manual content curation. |

**Junior + Ayrshare = the only tool that goes from raw video → viral clips → 10 platforms automatically.**
